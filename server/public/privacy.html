Privacy Policy

App: StarCallLive · Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StarCallLive (“we”, “us”, the “App”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our video-calling application. By creating an account you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address, display name, and password you provide when you register. Passwords are stored only as a salted hash — we never store your plain password.
  • Profile information (hosts): optional bio and per-minute rate you choose to publish.
  • Purchase records: when you buy coins we record the Google Play order ID, product ID, and coins credited. Payment card details are handled entirely by Google Play — we never see them.
  • Call metadata: the participants, start/end time, duration, and coins spent for each call. We do not record, store, or transmit the audio or video content of your calls; video and audio are streamed peer-to-peer/relayed only for the live duration of the call.
  • Safety data: reports and blocks you create, so we can review abuse and keep blocked users apart.
  • Technical data: basic device/network information needed to route calls and secure the service.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the service: authentication, matching callers with hosts, and connecting live calls.
  • To process coin purchases and credit your wallet, and to pay host earnings.
  • To keep the community safe: reviewing reports, enforcing blocks, and removing abusive accounts.
  • To detect fraud and comply with legal obligations.

3. Camera and microphone

The App requests camera and microphone permission solely to enable live video calls that you start or accept. These streams are used only during an active call and are not recorded by us.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us run the App (for example, our cloud hosting and Google Play billing) and where required by law. Your display name and profile are visible to other users as part of using a social calling app.

5. Data retention

We keep account and transaction records for as long as your account is active and as required for legal, accounting, and fraud-prevention purposes. You may request deletion of your account at any time (see below).

6. Your rights & account deletion

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us. When you delete your account we remove your profile and personal data, except records we are legally required to retain (such as purchase records).

7. Children

StarCallLive is intended only for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly allow anyone under 18 to use the App. If we learn that an underage person has registered, we will terminate the account.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
